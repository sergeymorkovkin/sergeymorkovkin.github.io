In the beginning of a project, the right technical decisions ultimately determine the success or failure of a project and sometimes business. And yet, many a time companies will wonder into a project treating the hard and consequential decisions often as an afterthought. I have heard time and time again, “If anything, we can just change it out later.” They do not realize that as time passes, the “change it out later” ends up being more expensive, or too time consuming, harmfully affects the business overall.

This is why I take the time to account every aspect within the technical management stage. In addition, my expertise will be amplified by a team of professional developers to make sure your business receives the very best technical management solutions at the core of your project.

The “Save now pay later” approach always ends up costing more in the end. The most important technical decisions are taken during the project initiation phase. This is why, when it comes to development, I focus on the core of the product from a long-term perspective, helping to make the important and hard technical decisions at the early stages of the project.

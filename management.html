software development teams

Custom-built development team service provides unique advantages for a software production business. These benefits range from maximizing on tax breaks to utilizing IT specialists for a fraction of the price you would normally pay in western countries.

Without strategic guidance, however, the process of outsourcing or outstaffing your business can become a headache. Before you can launch your development office, there are matters that need to be addressed:

  • Establish professional legal support and risk management
  • Oversee the recruitment process to hire the best candidates
  • Prevent staff turnover, by keeping the employees motivated
  • Organize office infrastructure from laptops to coffee
  • Provide an effective technical product management
